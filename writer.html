Writer, Rebel, Soldier, Lover: The Many Lives of Agyeya

Author: Akshaya Mukul

Alice Walker

A fascinating biography that delves deep into the multifaceted life of Agyeya. Akshaya Mukul has done an excellent job of portraying the complexities of his character. A gripping read from start to finish.
